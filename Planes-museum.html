Home to one of the largest Commemorative Air Force units in the world.

The Airzona Commemorative Air Force Museum, a.k.a Airbase Arizona Aircraft Museum, was established in 1978, in Falcon Field in Mesa, Arizona. It is the 10th unit of the Commemorative Air Force and the home of one of the largest Commemorative Air Force units in the world.
