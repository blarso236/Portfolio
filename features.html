About this


Cybernation is a store in which you can (and will) upgrade
your own body. Whether you want to get a new arm, a
better eye (that isn't fragile), or even get an implant
injected into your own bloodstream with your own custom
upgrades, you can find anything your body desires. The
future is not far from here, and it's time for the
Cybernation to rise.



















Shipping Policies


Creating this next level tech is expensive, but to ship all of
this, we here at Cybernation have to go through with
picking the right size box and making sure it is in perfect
working condition so that this equipment doesn't
get damaged along the way. To keep everything in perfect
condition, our Shipping fees are a bit higher than average
at 12%.



















WARNING!

• Returning our products is not possible.
• If you don't like the cybernetic arm, it's not like we can just reattach your arm.
• Cybernetic enhancements to your eye from us can be reversed, but only if you are willing to endure surgery.
• Taking out nanomachines is a lot harder than you'd think, and can only be removed within the first 2 weeks after purchase.
